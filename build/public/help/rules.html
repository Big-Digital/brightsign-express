OVERVIEW
In American Roulette, the goal is to predict where the ball will land on the spinning wheel. With its iconic double-zero layout, this version adds an extra layer of excitement and challenge.
The wheel features 38 numbered pockets: 1 to 36 (red and black), plus 0 and 00 (green). You can place a wide range of bets, from single numbers to groups of numbers, and even neighbor and racetrack bets.
Enjoy a smooth and immersive experience with adjustable game speed, a picture-in-picture wheel spin feature, and your choice of casino-themed background music. Whether you're a casual spinner or a seasoned Player, Signature American Roulette delivers fast-paced, high-stakes entertainment every round.
PLAY THE GAME
1. Place Your Bets
Begin play by selecting a chip value. To place a bet, simply select your desired numbers or betting zones on the table layout.
Bets can be placed on:
• Single numbers (Straight)
• Groups of numbers (Split, Street, Corner, Top Line, Six Numbers)
• Outside bets (Column, Dozen, Odd / Even, Red / Black, 1 to 18 / 19 to 36)
2. Spin the Wheel
Once all bets are placed, the Dealer spins the wheel in one direction and spins the ball in the opposite direction.
3. Winning Number Revealed
When the ball lands in a compartment, the winning number and color are announced. All losing bets are collected, winning bets are paid according to the Paytable.
4. Repeat
Players can place new bets for the next round.
HOUSE RULES
GAMEPLAY
• Wheel Layout: American Roulette is played on a double-zero wheel that contains 38 equally spaced compartments for the ball to land in. This setup includes one compartment marked 0 (green), one marked 00 (green), and thirty-six compartments numbered 1 to 36, which alternate in colors of red and black.
• Number Board:
• The board must accommodate 38 numbers; 1 to 36, 0, and 00.
• Grid layout:
• Numbers 1 to 36 are arranged in 12 rows and 3 columns.
• The 0 and 00 are placed above or to the left of the grid, depending on device display orientation.
• How to Place and Manage Bets:
• Tap a Bet Spot: Press your desired spot on the table to place a chip of the selected value. Each tap adds another chip of the selected value. As your stack grows, chips may automatically “color up” to represent higher denominations.
• Repeat Last Bet:
• Tap the REBET button to repeat your previous bet without initiating the wager. You can add more bets on top if you wish.
• Tap the REBET & SPIN button to repeat your previous bet and initiate the wager.
• Double Your Bets: After placing one or more bets, press the BET 2X button to instantly double all current bets.
• To reverse your last betting action - whether it involves placing or clearing one or more bets - simply press the UNDO button.
• Tap the CLEAR button to remove all newly placed bets from the board in one go.
• Types of Bets:
• Inside Bets: Place a bet (selected chip) on specific numbers or to cover adjacent numbers:
• Straight - chip placed on a single number, placed directly on the number box.
• Split - chip placed on two adjacent numbers, placed on the line separating the number boxes.
• Street - chip placed on three numbers in a single row, placed on the line separating the number boxes and the 1st 12, 2nd 12, and 3rd 12 label bet boxes. A Street bet may also be placed on 0, 1, and 2; 0, 2, and 00; or 00, 2, and 3 by placing the chip on the common corner of the number boxes.
• Corner - chip placed on any one of four numbers in contiguous boxes, placed on the common corner of the number boxes.
• Top Line (First Five Numbers) - chip placed on five numbers (0, 00, 1, 2, or 3), placed on the common corner of the number boxes 0 and 1 and the 1st 12 label bet box.
• Six Line - chip placed on six consecutive numbers, contained in two contiguous rows on the number board, placed on the outside common corner of the first number box in each of the rows selected.
• Outside Bets: Place a bet to cover a broad group of numbers by simply tapping one of these category areas on the screen to highlight all the numbers it covers on the betting layout. Category areas:
• Column - chip placed on column 1 2:1 (1, 4, 7, ..., 34), column 2 2:1 (2, 5, 8, ..., 35), or column 3 2:1 (3, 6, 9, ..., 36) number boxes.
• Dozen - chip placed on one of the 1st 12, 2nd 12, or 3rd 12 label bet boxes.
• Odd / Even - chip placed on all ODD or all EVEN numbers.
• Red / Black - chip placed on all Red (red diamond bet box) or all Black (black diamond bet box) numbers.
• 1 to 18 / 19 to 36 - chip placed on all low (1 to 18) or all high (19 to 36) number bet label boxes.
• Neighbor Bets:
• Open the Racecourse Board: To access the racecourse betting interface, simply press the racecourse button.
• Highlight Neighboring Numbers: Hover over any number on the racecourse oval to highlight it, along with its immediate neighbors on both sides.
• Place a Bet: To place your chosen chip value, simply click on a number within the racecourse oval. This action will allocate your chip to that number as well as its adjacent neighbors. Please note that the chips will be displayed on the main number board rather than on the oval itself.
• Adjust Neighbors: To adjust the number of neighboring numbers included in your bet, simply use the plus (+) and minus (-) buttons located on the racecourse oval. The displayed number indicates the number of neighbors selected.
• Favorite Bets: To save your current bet, simply click the heart button. Once the game launches, enter your preferred bet name and select Save. Your bet will then be displayed in the list. To place a saved bet, just click on the bet name from the list. If you want to remove any saved bets, click the delete button next to the respective bet name.
• Bet Dialog: To access the Bet Dialog, simply long-press on the bet box area. This dialog will show you the size of the current bet, along with the minimum (MIN) and maximum (MAX) betting limits. Additionally, you will find options to place a new bet, double (2X) your current bet, or delete the existing bet.
• Bet Tap & Drag: The Bet Tap & Drag feature enables you to position a chip on a designated bet spot and then seamlessly drag the cursor across other betting boxes, expanding your wager to encompass additional bet areas according to the rules of the game. The feature is only available on mobile devices.
OTHER RULES:
• Statistics Window
• Game history statistics for the current session can be accessed by clicking the bar chart button:
• Winning Number Frequency Distribution Wheel: Track the frequency of each number being a winning number during the game session.
• Track Hot and Cold Numbers: Track the five most frequently hit numbers ("Hot Numbers") and the five least frequently hit numbers ("Cold Numbers") are displayed.
• Game History Grid: Track the last 14 winning numbers, with the most recently hit numbers populating from the right. Top row is Black, middle row is Green, bottom row is Red.
